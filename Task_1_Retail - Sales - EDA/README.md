# 🛒 Retail Sales Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset using Python. The objective is to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify product performance, analyze customer demographics, and uncover business insights through statistical analysis and data visualization.

The analysis was performed using Jupyter Notebook and Python libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Explore the structure and quality of the dataset.
- Perform descriptive statistical analysis.
- Analyze customer demographics.
- Identify outliers in purchase amounts.
- Understand product category distribution.
- Find the top-selling products by revenue.
- Analyze seasonal purchasing patterns.
- Study relationships between numerical features using a correlation heatmap.
- Generate actionable business recommendations from the findings.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The dataset contains no missing values.
- Purchase Amount contains several high-value outliers.
- Male and female customer counts are nearly equal.
- Customers are distributed fairly evenly across the age range of 20–70 years.
- Footwear and Sports categories have the highest customer purchases.
- Electronics generate the highest average purchase amount.
- Laptops, Mobile Phones, Headphones, and Smart Watches contribute the highest revenue.
- Seasonal purchasing remains relatively stable throughout the year.
- Discount Applied and Previous Purchases show a strong positive correlation.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Focus on High-Revenue Products
Increase inventory and promotional efforts for high-performing products such as laptops, mobile phones, headphones, and smart watches.

### 2. Improve Customer Retention
Customers with more previous purchases tend to receive higher discounts. Expanding loyalty and reward programs can encourage repeat purchases.

### 3. Optimize Product Categories
Continue investing in Electronics, Sports, and Footwear categories while evaluating lower-performing categories for improvement.

### 4. Expand Premium Product Marketing
Electronics generate the highest average purchase amount. Personalized promotions and premium bundles can further increase revenue.
